about me

hi! i'm gavin. i'm a university of manitoba computer science student.

my interest in photography was sparked in 2018 when i was gifted a canon rebel t6. since then, i've expanded my
camera collection to include a kodak easyshare Z885 digital camera, a minolta stsi maxxum film camera, and the
polaroid sun 660 instant film camera, which these photos are taken with.

i'm working to be the best programmer i can be, so maintaining this website will be a start. this website's repository
is available on my github, and the link is on the contact page. take a look if you're interested.

i hope you enjoy my website, it was adapted from a template by nicepage.com
